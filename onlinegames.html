Highway Traffic

The aim of the Highway Traffic game is to drive down the road without hitting other cars. This can be tricky as other cars are always changing speed, slowing down, speeding up, or stopping suddenly.

Pay attention and be ready to make quick decisions. Highway Traffic gets harder (and more enjoyable) as you progress. Don't hesitate, hit the road now!

Crazy Strike Force

Crazy Strike Force is a multiplayer first person shooter game for battle enthusiasts. You do not need expensive consoles and licenses to enjoy a realistic battle experience. High quality visuals and sound effects will keep you captivated. With a great selection of weapons, you will be an ultimate shooter.

Drift Hunters Pro

Drift Hunters Pro is an exciting game with high-quality graphics that caters to those who love drifting and racing. You can either play solo or join other players on the servers. The game offers a range of features including six different tracks and ten upgradable vehicles in the garage. Get ready for an adrenaline-fueled tire track experience!

LOLBeans io

LOLbeans io is a fun online racing game similar to famous console game Fall Guys. Take control of a cute jelly bean, and compete against others in a battle royale.

The game is colorful and amusing, with plenty of obstacles to overcome. Try out LOLbeans io and see if you can reach to the top of the leaderboard!

BitLife

BitLife is a game that simulates life. You will play as different people from birth to death, making important choices on their behalf. 

You can help your character become a model citizen with a successful career and social life. Or you can choose to play as a criminal and see what he is capable of. Possibilities are limitless. You may even marry into royalty by working on your charisma!

Need a break from your own life? Start a new journey in BitLife!
